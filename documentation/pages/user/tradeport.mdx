# List Names

[TradePort](https://www.tradeport.xyz/iota/collection/0x6d2c743607ef275bd6934fe5c2a7e5179cca6fbd2049cfa79de2310b74f3cf83%3A%3Aname_registration%3A%3ANameRegistration?bottomTab=trades&tab=items) is an NFT marketplace where you can list and trade your IOTA Names. This guide walks you through connecting your wallet and listing a name for sale.

## Prerequisites

- Your IOTA wallet with at least one registered IOTA Name
- Sufficient IOTA tokens to cover any gas fees

## Step-by-Step Guide

1. Go to the [IOTA Names collection on TradePort](https://www.tradeport.xyz/iota/collection/0x6d2c743607ef275bd6934fe5c2a7e5179cca6fbd2049cfa79de2310b74f3cf83%3A%3Aname_registration%3A%3ANameRegistration?bottomTab=trades&tab=items). You will see all currently listed names along with market stats such as Floor Price, Volume and the number of listed Items.
    ![TradePort IOTA Names collection](/images/tradeport/tradeport-collection.png)
1. Click **Connect Wallet** in the top-right corner to get started and approve the connection.
1. Once connected, click the **My Items** tab in the IOTA Names collection page. You will see all IOTA Names held in your connected wallet.
1. Hover over a name to see the **List** button. Click it to open the list modal.
    ![List button on hover](/images/tradeport/tradeport-list.png)
1. Enter your desired listing price in IOTA then click **List NFT** to submit the listing. Your wallet will prompt you to approve the transaction.
    ![Listing price modal](/images/tradeport/tradeport-list-modal.png)

After confirming the transaction, your IOTA Name will be visible to buyers browsing the IOTA Names collection on TradePort.
