# Linked Address

You can change the address that your IOTA name resolves to. The original address that registered the IOTA name retains control over its configuration, but the name points to the address you provide.

## Tell me how

To link an address to your IOTA name:

1. Tap or click the **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right. If your wallet is already connected, your address or IOTA name appears here instead.
1. Follow the prompts to connect your IOTA Wallet, if not already connected.
1. After connecting, the **Connect Wallet** button displays your address (or connected IOTA default name). Tap or click the button to open the account dialog.
1. Tap or click the **My Names** button in the dialog. The **Names** view opens with a list of names associated with the connected account.
1. Tap the icon for the name you want to change and click the **three dots settings button**. This will view the name's details and options.
1. Select **Connect to Address** from the menu.
1. In the text box named **Enter Address**, type the address you want the name to resolve to. This address must be a valid IOTA address. You can also use your current address by clicking the **Use current** 
1. Save the changes by clicking the **Save** button, and approve the transaction using your connected wallet.
1. Go to the [IOTA Explorer](https://explorer.iota.org/) and search for your name. It should direct you to your IOTA address.
