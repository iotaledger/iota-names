## Tell me how

To buy an IOTA name:

1. Go to the [IOTA-Names site](https://iotanames.com/) and tap or click the search bar (**Search for your IOTA name**).
1. Type the name you want in **Check name availability**.
1. If the name is available, tap or click **Buy** on the result card.
1. In the **Register name** dialog, review the details. You can choose the registration duration and optionally enable **Set name as Public Name**.
1. Tap or click **Buy** and approve the transaction in your wallet.
1. After the transaction completes, the name appears under **My Names**.
