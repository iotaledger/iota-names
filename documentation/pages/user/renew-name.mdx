## Tell me how

To renew an IOTA name:

1. Tap or click the **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right and connect your wallet.
1. Open **My Names** and find the name you want to renew.
1. Tap the name card, then open the **three dots settings button**.
1. Select **Renew Name**.
1. In the **Renew Name** dialog, choose the renewal period and review the new expiration date.
1. Tap or click **Renew** and approve the transaction in your wallet.
