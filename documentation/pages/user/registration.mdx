# Register Names

For now, name registrations happen through auctions. You start an auction by placing the opening bid, others can bid while the auction is active, and the winner claims the name.

## Auction basics

- Auctions run for at least 48 hours and extend if a bid is placed in the final 10 minutes.
- Your bid must be at least the minimum shown in the dialog.
- If someone outbids you, your previous bid is refunded.
- The 1-year subscription starts at the auction's start time.

1. Go to the [IOTA-Names site](https://testnet.iotanames.com/) and click **Start Auction** under **Live Auctions** (or use the search bar).
1. Type the name you want and run the search.
1. If the name can be auctioned, tap or click **Bid** on the result card to open the auction dialog.
1. Enter your opening bid (at least the minimum shown) and tap **Start auction**.
1. Approve the transaction in your wallet.
1. After the transaction completes, the name appears under **My Names** > **In Auction**.
