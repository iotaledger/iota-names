## Tell me how

To assign an owned NFT to your IOTA name:

1. Tap or click the **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right. 
  
    ![Connect button](/images/ui/connect-btn.png)
    If your wallet is already connected, your address or IOTA name appears here instead.
1. Follow the prompts to connect your IOTA Wallet, if not already connected.
1. After connecting, the **Connect Wallet** button displays your address (or connected IOTA default name). Tap or click the button to open the account dialog.
  ![Dialog](/images/ui/view-names.png)
1. Tap or click the **Your Names** button in the dialog. The **Names** view opens with a list of names associated with the connected account. 
1. Tap the icon for the name you want to change and click **Manage**. This will view the name's details and options.
  ![Manage name](/images/ui/manage-name.png)
1. Select **Update Avatar NFT** from the menu to open the avatar dialog. 
1. The dialog lists the NFTs for your address. Select the one you want to use and click **Update Avatar**.
1. Save the changes by clicking the **Save** button, and approve the transaction using your connected wallet.
