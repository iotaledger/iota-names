# Update Avatars

Have a special NFT that you want to represent your identity on chain? After you [buy a name](./buy-without-auction.mdx), you can set the avatar to associate with that name.

## Tell me how

To assign an owned NFT to your IOTA name:

1. Tap or click the **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right. If your wallet is already connected, your address or IOTA name appears here instead.
1. Follow the prompts to connect your IOTA Wallet, if not already connected.
1. After connecting, the **Connect Wallet** button displays your address (or connected IOTA default name). Tap or click the button to open the account dialog.
1. Tap or click the **My Names** button in the dialog. The **Names** view opens with a list of names associated with the connected account. 
1. Tap the icon for the name you want to change and click on the **three dots settings button**. This will view the name's options.
1. Select **Personalize Avatar** from the menu to open the avatar dialog. 
1. The dialog lists the NFTs for your address. Select the one you want to use and click **Save**.
1. Approve the transaction using your connected wallet.
