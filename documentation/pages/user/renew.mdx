# Renew Names

You can renew, or extend the expiration date, for any of your registered names for up to five years.

## Tell me how

To renew registered names:

1. Tap or click the **Connect Wallet** button in the upper right. 
  
    ![Connect button](/images/ui/connect-btn.png)
    If your wallet is already connected, your address or IOTA name appears here instead.
1. Follow the prompts to connect your IOTA Wallet, if not already connected.
1. After connecting, the **Connect Wallet** button displays your address (or connected IOTA default name). Tap or click the button to open the account dialog.
  ![Dialog](/images/ui/view-names.png)
1. Tap or click the **View names** button in the dialog. The **Names** view opens with a list of names associated with the connected account. 
1. Select a name that you want to renew by clicking **Manage**.
1. Select **Renew** from the menu to open the dialog.
1. Use the **Input renew years** field to set the number of years to retain the rights to the name. The maximum value is five years minus the current registration period. So, if you just registered the name, the maximum number of years you can renew is four. As you increase or decrease the value, IOTA-Names updates the amount in IOTA and the registration expiration date based on the value. 
    ![Extend](/images/ui/renew.png)
1. Tap or click **Extend Name Expiry** button. 
1. Approve the transaction using your connected wallet. 

### Renewal grace period

When the current date surpasses the last day of your registration period, the IOTA name expires. You have a thirty-day grace period within which you can renew the registration. After that, you must [register](./registration.mdx) the name again, if it's available.
