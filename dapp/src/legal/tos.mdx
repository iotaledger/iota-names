**2\. Service Description**

IOTA Names is a decentralised, smart contract-based name service that allows users to register human-readable names ending in ".iota", which resolve to their cryptographic addresses. Similar in concept to the DNS for websites, this service enables easier identification and communication on-chain by mapping complex wallet addresses with recognisable names. The system utilises non-fungible tokens (NFTs) to represent ownership of each name. These NFTs are transferable and valid for a limited registration period.

**3\. Subscription**

Name subscriptions may be purchased for a fee by connecting your wallet and completing a direct purchase, or via an auction process, as displayed in the Service.

All **.iota** names are issued as time-limited subscriptions rather than permanent ownership. The fee structure is based on the length of the name:

- **3-character names**: 1500 IOTA/year
- **4-character names**: 250 IOTA/year
- **5-character names**: 50 IOTA/year

Initially, IOTA tokens will be the only accepted form of payment. Future updates may enable additional payment options such as stablecoins or ecosystem tokens.

Network transaction fees, as set by the IOTA protocol, may vary and are applied accordingly. For any transaction you initiate, your estimated fees will be shown to you.

Each registered name is represented by a non-fungible token (NFT), which signifies ownership for the duration of the selected registration period. Users can choose to register a name for any term between one and five years. Once the term expires, the NFT becomes invalid, unless the registration is renewed.

To prevent accidental loss of ownership, the system includes a 30-day grace period after expiration. During this time, the current owner retains the exclusive right to renew the name and reactivate the NFT. If the name is not renewed before the grace period ends, it becomes publicly available for others to register. You are solely responsible for monitoring your subscription’s expiration date and completing any necessary renewal to avoid losing your IOTA name.

**All subscription purchases are final and non-refundable**. Once a subscription has been paid for, it cannot be cancelled, and no refunds will be issued for any reason, including for unused portions of the subscription period.
